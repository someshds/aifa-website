💡 Pro Tips to Get More Reviews
Simple tactics that the top-rated businesses use.
⏰
Ask within 24 hours. The closer to the experience, the more likely they'll leave a review. Send the SMS or email the same day.
🎯
Make it personal. "Hi Sarah, it was great helping with your boiler today — would you mind leaving us a quick review?" beats generic requests every time.
📱
Use the QR code everywhere. Print it on receipts, invoices, business cards, van signage, and your front desk. The easier you make it, the more reviews you'll get.
💬
Always respond to reviews. Google rewards businesses that engage. Reply to every review within 48 hours — yes, even the bad ones.
🚫
Never offer incentives for reviews. It violates Google's policies and can get your listing suspended. Just ask genuinely — most happy customers are glad to help.
