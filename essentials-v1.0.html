Common questions

Do I need any tech skills to use this?+
None at all. We set everything up for you during your onboarding call and walk you through the mobile app. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use this. And we're always on hand if you need help.
What does "usage-based" SMS & email costs mean?+
Your £97/mo subscription includes the full platform. SMS and email sends are billed separately based on what you actually use. For a typical sole trader, this works out to about £5–£15/mo. We'll give you a full breakdown during onboarding.
How does the missed call text-back work?+
When a call comes in and you can't answer, the system automatically sends a text to the caller within seconds — something like "Sorry I missed your call — how can I help?" The message is fully customisable. It keeps the lead warm while you're finishing your current job.
Will this work with my existing phone number?+
Yes. We can set up call forwarding from your existing number, or you can use a new dedicated business number alongside it. Your current customers won't notice any change.
What if I want marketing features later?+
You can upgrade to our Growth plan at any time to unlock email/SMS marketing campaigns, landing pages, AI webchat, social media scheduling, and advanced automations. No data migration needed — everything carries over seamlessly.
Is there a contract?+
No long-term contracts. It's a rolling monthly subscription — cancel any time. You get a 14-day free trial to make sure it works for you before paying a penny.
What trades is this built for?+
Chimney sweeps, plumbers, electricians, cleaners, landscapers, roofers, HVAC engineers, builders, painters, handymen — any sole trader or small home-service business. The templates and pipeline are pre-configured for how tradespeople actually work.
