Common questions

Do I need any tech skills?+
No. We set everything up for you. Your 60-minute onboarding call covers the full walkthrough — AI Voice setup, invoicing, workflows, and reporting. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use this.
What are the AI Voice usage costs?+
AI Voice minutes are charged per minute of call time. Combined with SMS and email usage, typical total costs run £30–£60/mo for an established business. We give you a full breakdown during onboarding so there are no surprises.
Does the AI receptionist sound natural?+
Yes. Modern AI voices are remarkably natural and conversational. We customise the receptionist with your business name, services, pricing guidance, service areas, and tone of voice. Most callers won’t realise they’re speaking to AI.
Can I keep my existing phone number?+
Absolutely. We set up call forwarding from your existing number, so your current customers don’t notice any change. You can also use a new dedicated number alongside your existing one if you prefer.
How does the quote-to-payment workflow work?+
A customer enquiry triggers an automated quote. Once accepted, booking confirmation goes out. When you mark the job as complete, an invoice is generated with an online payment link. If payment is overdue, automatic reminders are sent. The entire flow runs itself — with manual overrides wherever you need them.
Can I integrate with my accounting software?+
Yes. We support integrations with Xero, QuickBooks, Google Sheets, and many more. During your onboarding call, we’ll scope exactly which integrations your business needs and build them for you.
Is there a contract?+
No long-term contracts. It’s a rolling monthly subscription — cancel any time. You get a 14-day free trial to make sure it works for your business before paying a penny.
What’s the difference between Growth and Dominate?+
Dominate includes everything in Growth, plus six additional capabilities: AI Voice receptionist (24/7 call answering), full quote-to-payment automation, invoicing with online payments, advanced reporting dashboard, custom automations and integrations, and priority support with monthly strategy calls.
