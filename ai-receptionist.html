Common questions

Does it actually sound natural?+
Yes. Modern AI voices are remarkably natural — most callers don't realise they're speaking to an AI. We fine-tune the voice, tone, and pacing to match your brand personality.
What happens with calls the AI can't handle?+
Complex or sensitive queries are seamlessly transferred to you or your team with full context — the AI provides a summary of the conversation so your team can pick up without the caller repeating themselves.
Can I use it just for after-hours calls?+
Absolutely. You can configure call forwarding rules — for example, only forward to the AI after 6pm, on weekends, or when your line is busy. You're in full control.
How does appointment booking work?+
The AI connects to your existing calendar (Google Calendar, Outlook, or our built-in CRM) and shows available slots to the caller. When they pick a time, it's booked instantly and both you and the caller get confirmation.
Do I need to change my phone number?+
No. You keep your existing business number. We set up call forwarding from your current number to the AI receptionist — or you can use a new dedicated number alongside your existing one.
Is there a contract or minimum term?+
No long-term contracts. It's a rolling monthly subscription — cancel any time with 30 days' notice. We're confident you'll want to keep it once you see the results.
