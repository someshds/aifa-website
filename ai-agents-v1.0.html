Common questions

What exactly is an AI agent?+
An AI agent is software that can perform tasks autonomously — like answering customer queries, processing documents, or managing your calendar. Unlike simple chatbots, agents can make decisions, use tools, and handle multi-step workflows.
Do I need technical knowledge?+
Not at all. We handle everything — design, build, integration, and training. You just tell us what tasks you want automated, and we build the agent to do it.
What tools can agents connect to?+
We integrate with 2,000+ platforms including Google Workspace, Xero, QuickBooks, Stripe, Outlook, Slack, and most popular business tools. If your tool has an API, we can connect to it.
How long before I see results?+
Most clients see measurable time savings within the first week of their agent going live. Our Quick Win package delivers a working agent in just 2 weeks.
Is my data safe?+
Absolutely. We follow strict data handling practices, comply with UK GDPR, and your data never leaves your existing systems. We connect to your tools — we don't store your data.
What if the agent makes a mistake?+
Every agent has human-in-the-loop guardrails. For critical tasks, the agent flags decisions for your approval before acting. We monitor accuracy and continuously improve.
Can I start small and add more agents later?+
Yes — that's exactly what we recommend. Start with one Quick Win agent, see the results, then expand to a full AI Operating System when you're ready.
