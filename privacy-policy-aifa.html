3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal bases (UK GDPR):

  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a)): When you submit a contact form, book a call, or opt in to receive marketing emails. You give explicit consent by submitting the form or ticking a consent checkbox.
  • Legitimate Interest (Article 6(1)(f)): For anonymous website analytics to improve our services and user experience.
  • Contract Performance (Article 6(1)(b)): When we process data necessary to deliver services you have purchased.

You can withdraw consent at any time (see Section 7).

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide requested information
  • To send personalised reports (e.g. ROI Calculator results) via email
  • To schedule and conduct consultation calls
  • To deliver services you have purchased
  • To send occasional emails about AI automation tips and services (only if you have consented)
  • To improve our website and tools (aggregated, non-identifying analysis only)
  • To measure the effectiveness of our advertising (via anonymised tracking pixels)

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

5. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data may be stored in the following systems:

  • GoHighLevel (CRM): Hosted in the United States. Used for contact management, communications, and appointment scheduling.
  • Google Analytics: Hosted in the United States/EU. Anonymous usage data only.
  • Facebook (Meta): Hosted in the United States/EU. Anonymous advertising measurement data only.

Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses and the provider's compliance with applicable data protection frameworks. By consenting, you acknowledge this transfer under UK GDPR Article 49(1)(a).

6. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Contact form submissions: Retained for as long as you remain a contact in our CRM or until you request deletion.
  • Client data: Retained for 6 years after the end of the business relationship (for legal and accounting purposes).
  • Marketing contacts: Retained for as long as you remain subscribed. If you withdraw consent or request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.
  • Analytics data: Retained according to the respective service provider's retention policies (typically 14-26 months for Google Analytics).

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the UK General Data Protection Regulation, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing — ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to withdraw consent — withdraw your consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data based on legitimate interests

To exercise any of these rights, email grant@aifusionautomations.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe your data rights have been violated.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses the following tracking technologies:

  • Google Tag Manager (GTM-5JSZNZ4Q): Manages analytics and marketing tags. GTM itself does not collect personal data but loads scripts that may.
  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymous website usage data. Uses cookies to distinguish unique users. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Facebook Pixel (630094975903061): Tracks page views and conversion events for advertising measurement. You can control Facebook ad tracking in your Facebook Ad Preferences.
  • GoHighLevel Chat Widget: May set cookies to maintain chat sessions. Only activated when you interact with the chat.

Our standalone free tools pages are designed to minimise tracking. Calculations and data processing happen locally in your browser.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
