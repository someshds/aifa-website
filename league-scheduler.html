📖 How to Use
Men's Doubles League Box 3 — quick guide
👥 Teams Tab
1
The Teams tab shows all 6 doubles pairs in your league. Each card shows both players side by side.
2
Tap a player's photo circle to upload a profile photo. It's stored on your device and shows throughout the app.
3
New teams can be added with + Add Team, up to 6 teams. Once all teams are added, generate the round-robin fixtures.
💡 The league runs a full round-robin — every team plays every other team once (15 matches total).
📅 Availability Tab
1
Select your name from the dropdown at the top.
2
A weekly grid appears. Tap any slot (morning / afternoon / evening) to toggle yourself as available. Green = you're free.
3
Use ◀ Prev / Next ▶ to navigate between weeks.
4
The All Players — Next 2 Weeks calendar below updates instantly as you tap. Coloured initials show who's free in each slot.
💡 Your availability is saved to this device. Ask everyone to fill theirs in so the smart scheduler can find match times.
🎾 Auto-Match Suggestions
1
Scroll down in the Availability tab to see Match Suggestions.
2
The app checks all unplayed fixtures and finds slots where all 4 players are available at the same time.
3
Each suggestion shows the match and date options. Head to Fixtures to propose or confirm those times.
💡 The more availability everyone fills in, the more suggestions appear. It looks up to 6 weeks ahead.
📋 Fixtures Tab
1
Every match has a status badge. Use the filter dropdown to view matches by status.
Unscheduled Pending Scheduled Played
2
Tap any match to open its detail panel. From there you can propose a time, confirm, or reschedule.
3
Once a match is played, tap Mark as Played and enter the set scores (e.g. 6-4 6-3 for each team).
💡 A green badge shows how many overlapping availability slots exist for that fixture — great for planning!
🏆 League Table & Scoring
1
1 point per set won + 1 bonus point for playing (awarded to both teams). A 2–0 win = 3pts. A 2–1 win = 3pts. A 1–2 loss = 2pts. A 0–2 loss = 1pt.
2
Walkovers: winning pair gets 2pts, other gets 0. Submit as League Points 2–0 with 0–0 in sets.
3
Tiebreaker order: Total points → Set difference (SF–SA) → Game difference. Columns: P played · W/D/L · SF/SA sets for/against · Pts.
💡 You must play at least 3 matches to qualify for promotion. Scroll below the table for the full results log.
📜 League Rules
1
Format: Best of 3 sets. If sets reach 1–1, play a 10-point championship tiebreak instead of a full third set.
2
Arranging matches: Propose at least 5 dates to your opponents before you can claim a walkover. Start early — deadline pressure is real!
3
Rain: If it rains before the match starts, reschedule the whole match. If it rains during play, resume from the exact score when conditions allow.
4
Promotion: Minimum 3 matches played to qualify. Top finishers are eligible for promotion at the end of the season.
💡 Use the Chat tab and Availability tab to coordinate match times early — don't leave it to the last week!
💬 Group Chat
1
Select your name from the dropdown so your messages are labelled correctly.
2
Type your message and tap Send (or press Enter). Your messages appear on the right in blue.
3
Chat is stored on this device — great for coordinating match times when everyone's using the same shared link.
💡 Use chat to confirm match times, share court booking links, or just trash-talk your opponents 😄
