Certified AI Partners

Anthropic opens a certified AI partner ecosystem to 40,000 firms.

A new Services Track and Partner Hub creates searchable, tiered access to certified Claude implementation partners — making qualified AI help findable for businesses of any size.

3 JunSource date 2026
4 minPractical read
1Signal

The short version: Anthropic has created a structured, searchable directory of certified AI implementation partners. UK businesses can now find qualified Claude consultants without guessing.

What Anthropic announced

Anthropic launched two new components inside its Claude Partner Network: a Services Track and a Partner Hub. The Services Track creates a formal pathway for consultancies, agencies, and individual practitioners to become certified Claude implementation partners. The Partner Hub is the searchable directory where businesses can find them.

The network uses three tiers: Select, Preferred, and Global Premier. Each tier reflects the depth of Anthropic's assessment, the volume of certified practitioners, and the level of backing from Anthropic for partner-led client engagements. The programme currently has more than 10,000 certified consultants across participating firms, and Anthropic has committed $100M in programme support to help certified partners scale their practices.

The announcement positions certified partners as the implementation layer for enterprise and mid-market AI adoption — with smaller businesses implicitly in scope as partner practices grow and the lower tiers of the network fill in.

What this means for UK small businesses

Until now, finding a consultant who genuinely knew how to implement Claude-based AI in a business workflow was a matter of asking around, checking LinkedIn, or hoping a general IT agency had kept up with recent developments. The results were mixed, and the risk of paying for confident-sounding advice that did not reflect current model capabilities was real.

The Partner Hub changes that. A UK business owner can now search for certified implementation partners by geography, sector, or use case — and know that the firms listed have met Anthropic's training and assessment standards. That does not guarantee a good outcome on any individual project, but it does meaningfully reduce the guesswork around who to trust at the first meeting.

For businesses considering customer follow-up automation, inbox triage, proposal drafting, document processing, or CRM enrichment using Claude-based tools, a certified partner is the logical starting point. They have access to resources, support, and guidance that an uncertified agency or generalist consultant would not.
