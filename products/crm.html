The Problem

The lead that fell through the cracks

It's a familiar scene: a customer enquiry comes in by email, gets replied to on someone's phone, the follow-up sits in a Sent folder, the next conversation happens on WhatsApp, and a quote gets sent from a different inbox entirely. Three weeks later, you can't remember whether you ever heard back — and neither can your team.

The result, across the small-business landscape, is brutal: around half of all qualified leads never get a second touch. Not because the lead was bad, but because nobody had a single place to look and see “here's everyone in the middle of a conversation with us right now.”

Spreadsheets help for a few weeks, then collapse under their own weight. Sticky notes get binned. The inbox becomes the CRM, and the inbox is a terrible CRM. By the time you're juggling three jobs at once, leads are slipping out faster than you can replace them.

A real CRM closes that gap — not by adding admin, but by doing the admin for you. Every call, email, text, form fill, and chat captured automatically against the contact, with a clear view of where every deal sits in your pipeline.

What We Build

A white-label CRM that fits the way you already work

Our CRM is built on the GoHighLevel platform and configured from scratch for your business. We don't drop you into a generic template and wish you luck — we map your sales process, your custom fields, your tags, and your pipelines around how leads actually move through your business today.

Every conversation lands in one place. SMS, email, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram DMs, web chat, missed calls, voicemail transcripts — all flowing into a single unified inbox tied to the contact. One screen, every channel, no more app-switching.

On top of that sits a visual pipeline that shows exactly where every deal is, what it's worth, what the next step is, and when you last touched it. The CRM nudges you when a deal goes stale, scores leads automatically based on engagement, and surfaces the hottest ones at the top of your list every morning.

Common Questions

Things people ask before signing up

Will my existing data move across?

Yes — we handle the migration. CSV from your old CRM, a Google Sheet, an exported MailChimp list, scraped-together contacts from email signatures, all of it. We deduplicate, map fields properly, and import with tags so you can find what came from where.

Is it really unlimited contacts?

Yes. No per-contact, per-record, or per-seat charges. Most CRMs trap you in a pricing tier that gets more expensive as your list grows; ours doesn't. Bring 5,000 contacts or 50,000 — same price.

Can my team use it on mobile?

Yes. A native mobile app lets your team manage contacts, pipelines, conversations, and tasks from their phone. We can even white-label the app with your branding for an extra trust signal with customers.

Does it replace my email marketing tool?

Yes, if you want it to. Email broadcasts, drip sequences, segmented campaigns, automation triggers — all built in. If you'd rather keep Mailchimp/ActiveCampaign/etc., we can integrate instead.

What about phone calls and SMS?

Inbound and outbound calls are routed through a tracked business number, recorded, transcribed, and logged to the contact timeline. SMS is two-way and threaded with the rest of the conversation history.

Is it UK GDPR-compliant?

Yes. UK/EU data residency, encryption in transit and at rest, proper consent capture, retention controls, and full data subject access request support. Details in our privacy policy.

How long does setup take?

Most clients are fully live within 7–10 working days, including data migration, pipeline configuration, custom fields, integrations, and team training. Bigger or more complex businesses may take longer.
