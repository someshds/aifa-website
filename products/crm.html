Features

Contact Management

Unlimited contacts with custom fields, tags, and smart lists. Every interaction — calls, emails, texts, form submissions — logged automatically on the contact timeline.

📈

Sales Pipelines

Visual drag-and-drop pipelines that show exactly where every deal stands. Create multiple pipelines for different services, track deal values, and forecast revenue at a glance.

✓ Visual deal tracking

Lead Scoring

Automatic scoring based on engagement — email opens, link clicks, page visits, and form fills. Your hottest leads rise to the top so you always know who to call first.

✓ Prioritise hot leads

Conversation Hub

SMS, email, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram DMs, and web chat — all in one unified inbox. Reply from a single screen, no matter how your customers reach out.

✓ One inbox for everything

Smart Lists & Segmentation

Filter contacts by behaviour, tags, engagement score, location, or any custom field. Build dynamic lists that update automatically — perfect for targeted campaigns.

✓ Dynamic segmentation

Call Tracking

Track every inbound and outbound call. Calls are recorded, transcribed, and logged directly to the contact timeline — so your team never misses context on a lead.

✓ Recorded & logged

Task Management

Create tasks, assign them to team members, set due dates and reminders. Link tasks to contacts or deals so follow-ups never get forgotten.
