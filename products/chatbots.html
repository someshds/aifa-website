The Problem

The lead that arrived at 9:47 pm

A potential customer lands on your website at 9:47 pm. They’ve got a question. Maybe it’s about pricing, availability, what areas you cover, or whether you handle their specific situation. They want an answer right now — not tomorrow morning.

So they fill in your contact form. Or they don’t. 78% of website visitors leave without ever reaching out — not because they don’t need what you sell, but because the friction of waiting is just too much. They click off, open a competitor’s tab, and that’s the last you ever hear of them.

Even when they do fill in the form, what happens next? It pings into an inbox. Sits overnight. By the time you reply at 8:30 the next morning, they’ve already booked with someone else. Speed-to-lead studies consistently show that responding within 5 minutes makes you up to 100× more likely to convert that enquiry into a customer than waiting an hour. Wait overnight, and the game is essentially over.

This is the gap an AI chatbot closes — not by replacing your human team, but by making sure the conversation never has a chance to die.

Common Questions

Things people ask before signing up

Will the chatbot sound robotic or off-brand?

No. We train each chatbot on your tone of voice, your terminology, and your specific phrasing. If you describe your business as “friendly and no-nonsense”, that’s how the chatbot reads. We do a tone check together before launch and adjust until it sounds right.

What happens when the chatbot doesn’t know the answer?

It does one of two things: politely captures the visitor’s contact details with a promise that someone will get back to them, or escalates the conversation in real time to a member of your team via SMS or app notification. Either way, the lead never falls through the cracks.

We also surface every “I don’t know” in your monthly conversation analytics so we can keep training the chatbot to handle more on its own over time.

Can it book straight into my calendar?

Yes. We connect the chatbot to your calendar (Google, Outlook, Apple, or any system that supports standard calendar sync) and it offers live availability inside the conversation. The visitor picks a slot, gets a confirmation by email and SMS, and the booking lands in your diary instantly.

How long does setup take?

Most chatbots go live within 5–7 working days from the discovery call. The exact timeline depends on how many qualifying questions you need, how many integrations are involved, and how much existing FAQ content you have for us to work from.

Is my customer data safe?

Yes. All conversations are stored on UK and EU data centres, encrypted at rest and in transit. We follow UK GDPR requirements end to end — including proper consent capture, retention periods, and data subject access requests. Full details are in our privacy policy.

Do I need to use GoHighLevel for this to work?

No. GoHighLevel is the platform we use for most clients because it bundles CRM, calendar, payments, and messaging into one stack — but the chatbot can integrate with whatever you already use. If you’re on HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, or anything else, we’ll wire it up.

What does it cost?

Chatbots are part of our AI Operating System packages. Pricing depends on complexity, integrations, and the number of conversations expected per month. Have a look at our pricing page for the current tiers, or book a demo and we’ll quote your exact setup.
