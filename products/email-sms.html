The Problem

The list you've built but never properly used

You've been collecting email addresses for years. Customers from old jobs, leads who didn't convert, newsletter signups, podcast listeners, free-download grabbers. Somewhere on your laptop or in your CRM, there’s a list of thousands of people who at one point raised a hand — and you've barely emailed any of them.

Why? Because email feels hard. You don’t know if your domain is set up to land in the inbox. You don’t know what to send. You worry about hitting unsubscribe en masse. You opened Mailchimp once, got confused, and closed it.

Meanwhile, every month that list sits dormant is leaked revenue. An average past-customer is roughly 7× more likely to buy again than a cold prospect — and they cost you nothing to reach. SMS hits open rates of 95%+. Email still returns £36 for every £1 spent industry-wide. You’re sitting on the highest-return marketing asset you own.

What We Build

Email and SMS infrastructure that actually gets used

We set up your sending domain properly: SPF, DKIM, DMARC, dedicated subdomain, warm-up sequence — so your emails land in the inbox, not the Promotions tab or the Junk folder. Then we plug your CRM into a unified messaging engine that handles broadcasts, drip sequences, transactional emails, and two-way SMS in one place.

Segmentation is built-in: send to everyone who booked in the last 90 days, or everyone within a postcode radius, or everyone who hasn't engaged in 6 months. Smart lists update automatically based on contact behaviour — no manual list rebuilding before every send.

And we build you the 5 core campaigns every service business should have: a welcome sequence, a quote follow-up, a review request flow, a re-engagement campaign, and a seasonal recurring revenue play. Done once, running forever.

Common Questions

Things people ask before signing up

Will my emails land in the inbox?

Yes — we handle the deliverability set-up properly. Custom sending domain, SPF, DKIM, DMARC alignment, IP reputation management, content scanning. We also do a controlled warm-up so your domain reputation builds correctly instead of cold-blasting and getting marked as spam.

Do I need a separate SMS provider?

No — SMS is built in. UK shortcode and long-code numbers, two-way conversations, opt-out handling, and reply detection all native. Same dashboard, same contact records, same automation engine.

How does it handle GDPR consent?

Proper consent capture at every entry point (form, opt-in, double opt-in for sensitive lists). One-click unsubscribe in every email. SMS opt-out by replying STOP. Full audit trail of every consent action against the contact record. You stay compliant.

What about template design?

Drag-and-drop builder with mobile-first templates. We can also build you a brand-aligned template library so every send looks consistent without you having to design each one. Or, plain-text-style emails (which often perform better) — just type.

Can I send to specific segments?

Yes. Smart lists auto-update based on contact data and behaviour. Send to anyone who hasn't booked in 90 days, or anyone in a postcode radius, or anyone tagged as a particular service type, or anyone whose last email open was over 30 days ago.

What's the cost?

Email is unlimited on standard plans. SMS is metered by message because we pay UK carrier rates; current pricing on our pricing page. We help size the right SMS bundle based on your expected send volume.
