Online Booking Calendar

Let customers book directly from your website, social media, or a shared link. Syncs instantly with Google Calendar so you always know what’s coming.

Paid Appointments

Charge upfront when customers book. Collect deposits or full payment at the point of booking to dramatically reduce no-shows and cancellations.

Round-Robin Scheduling

Distribute bookings evenly across your team. Each new appointment is automatically assigned to the next available team member — fair and efficient.

Automated Reminders

SMS and email reminders sent automatically before every appointment. Customise the timing — 24 hours, 1 hour, or both. No more chasing clients.

Class & Group Booking

Perfect for yoga classes, workshops, and group sessions. Set capacity limits, allow multiple attendees per slot, and manage waitlists automatically.

Recurring Appointments

Set up repeat bookings for regular clients. Weekly, fortnightly, or monthly — once it’s in the system, it takes care of itself.

Calendar Sync

Two-way sync with Google Calendar, Outlook, and iCal. Changes in one place appear everywhere instantly, so you never double-book.

Custom Availability

Set your available hours per day, per service, and per team member. Block out lunch breaks, holidays, and prep time between appointments.
